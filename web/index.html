The Melbourne-based street wear label, Voyage was founded by Jean Stefano in an effort to offer high quality, durable, comfortable clothes which have been crafted in order to fit our constantly evolving, urban society. The brand’s aesthetic embodies its Australian roots, offering a range of modern designs. Each garment is a statement for any casual dress wardrobe, achieving a laid back look that’s comfortably cool. Voyage aims to create a catalogue which is up to date with the current urban trends and culture but gives you a new inspiration on life. We invite you to take this journey, to be independent, stand above the rest and realise one's dreams.
